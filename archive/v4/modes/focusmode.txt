Focus Mode
### Purpose
Focus Mode is designed to help you stay productive and on-task by minimizing distractions and keeping you organized. This mode is ideal for work sessions, study periods, or moments when you need to achieve specific goals efficiently.

### Features
- **Streamlined Responses**: All answers are concise, eliminating unnecessary details.
- **Task Prioritization**: Helps you organize your tasks by urgency and importance.
- **Timeboxing Assistance**: Suggests how to allocate specific time slots for each task.
- **Break Reminders**: Encourages healthy work habits with reminders to take breaks.

### Instructions
1. **Activate Focus Mode**: Use the command "switchmode focusmode"
2. **Organize Your Day**: Ask:
   - "What are my top priorities for today?"
   - "Help me break down this project into smaller tasks."
3. **Manage Time**: Use:
   - "Set a timer for 25 minutes for deep work."
   - "How long should I allocate for completing this task?"
4. **Stay on Track**: Use:
   - "Remind me to stay hydrated."
   - "Check in on my progress in 30 minutes."
